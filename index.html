aimara.coach is an AI-powered extension of the studio — a companion that carries aimara's methodology into every member's day. it learns their body, their habits, and their context, and coaches them accordingly.

the idea

beyond the studio walls.

aimara.studio has built one of Lisbon's most intentional wellness communities. but even the most committed members spend most of their lives outside the studio — making daily decisions about training, nutrition, and recovery without the context that makes those decisions meaningful.

aimara.coach changes that. it extends the studio experience into every member's day by combining objective biometric data from Whoop with voice-logged personal context, and reasoning through it all using aimara's own longevity methodology.

this is not a generic wellness app. it is a precision tool designed for a specific community of 100–200 members, grounded in a real philosophy — yours.

the process

built with aimara,
for aimara.

01

methodology first

before a line of code, we work with aimara's coaches to structure the studio's knowledge — protocols, values, nutrition philosophy — into the AI's foundation.

02
